Questions and Answers
Q: Do I need to change my Operating System to see the results ?
Ans: No. Nothing has to be changed in your local system.
Q: Do I need to be connected to internet ?
Ans: Yes. The server hosted is online and inorder to see results you must be connected to internet.
Q: Do I need to install any software / hardware ?
Ans: May be. If you are typing Assamese font then installing a typesetter in Assamese have to be done first.
Q: Do I need to learn any language ?
Ans: No. But for exact reading of phonetics(IPA) some or few phonetics sysmbols knowledge might be helpful.
Q: Can I record my audio ?
Ans: No. You cannot record your audio but can input voice message using the mic icon button on the mainpage.
Q: Can I download the voice results ?
Ans: Yes. The voice output can be downlaoded.
Q: Can I add words into the dataset myself ?
Ans: No. This service is not available now.
Q: Why I am not seeing any suggestions in words list ?
Ans: To see suggestions type into the textbox through the keyboard and the list appears.
Q: Why server error appears ?
Ans: This problem might be caused due to programming errors. If seen report at sarmah_mriganka@rediffmail.com
Q: Is this software copyright protected ?
Ans: Yes. Any unauthorized publishing or republishing of results without due credit to the authors is strictly punishable under law.
Q: Why no output is seen ?
Ans: Sometimes problems may be caused when server is updated. We Recommend to clear all your browsing history and continue. If problem persists contanct sarmah_mriganka@rediffmail
